Welcome to the official website for New Hope Centre

The New Hope Centre provides hope for the future in a nation that is dessimated by HIV/AIDS. Our mission is to provide a permanent home for orphaned and abandoned children in Swaziland, and to raise these children with love, education and a Biblical foundation, so that they are ready to lead Swaziland out of these difficult times and into a bright and safe future.
